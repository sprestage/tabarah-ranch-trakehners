Joriah


This 2008 mare has a lovely start in life. She was raised at home to learn her manners from grandmama and daily handling. 2011 saw Joriah started under saddle and her attendance at her first shows. She also matched her mother's performance at her own Trakehner inspection with a 54, also earning her the High Point award, and continuing to demonstrate a splendid example of our shared breeding goals of the ATA and the Trakehner Verband.


Here is Jori in one of her first days in the pasture as a foal. Next is 4 month old Jori at her foal evaluation at the 2008 ATA inspection.


Here is a short video of three year old Joriah free jumping a 3'3" vertical, December 2011.


Joriah won high point mare at her ATA inspection in Washington in 2011.
