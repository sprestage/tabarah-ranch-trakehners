Jädorin


This E.H. Herrzauber daughter has shown through 2nd level dressage and is now at home where she has joined her mother in the pasture where she is showing good promise as a broodmare. Jädorin's demonstrated our successful propagating of the goals set out by the ATA with her score of 54 earning her the High Point award at her Trakehner inspection in 2009.

We look forward to the 2012 inspection where we hope to present our first Jädorin daughter, Jakira.

Our congratulations and thanks to Meadowview for producing the fine stallions that grandsired (Nemo) and sired (E.H. Herrzauber) this beautiful lady.
